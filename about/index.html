About me

Telecommunications Engineer passionate about the ICT industry.

Working as a software engineer at Skyscanner. Currently based in Barcelona.

MSc. in Wireless Communication Systems at Aalborg University.
BSc. in Communication Systems Engineering at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid.

Attended four Universities in two countries working in multidisciplinary teams.

Previous professional experience

Ericsson, Spain

  • Internship at Ericsson R&D Madrid in the Testing and Continuous Integration team within the Software Defined Networks department.

MassM2M research group at Aalborg University, Denmark

Reseiwe, Denmark

  • Also during the Master, I had the chance to work part-time at a startup, Reseiwe, that spin-off from the MassM2M research group while completing my Master Thesis.
